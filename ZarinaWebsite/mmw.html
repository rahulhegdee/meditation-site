Meditation and Mindfulness Workshops


Zarina offers mindfulness workshops to organizations of all types, tailored to the needs of the group. The workshops run anywhere from 1.5 hours to a full day or two. These workshops allow participants to gain an understanding of the theory and practice of mindfulness and meditation, learn how to meditate both formally and informally, and practice proven stress reduction techniques. Mindfulness can support individuals in greater focus, concentration, and balance as they meet their goals at work and in life.
