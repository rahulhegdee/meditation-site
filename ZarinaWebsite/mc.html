Mind Clearing


Clearing is a one-on-one therapeutic approach which involves the premise that we all have withheld communications that cause pain and stress in our relationships and problems in our lives. In a clearing session, the client is given specific processes that create more insight, freedom, and ease. Clearing is excellent for tackling the day-to-day issues we face, using efficient, time-tested techniques of communicating and processing what we have been holding inside.
